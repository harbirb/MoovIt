Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 10, 2024

Welcome to MoovIt! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app. By using MoovIt, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

MoovIt does not collect any personal information apart from your Strava Athlete ID. We use this information to provide you with a personalized experience by matching your activities with relevant songs.

a. Authentication Data

To use MoovIt, you need to sign in to your Spotify and Strava accounts. We do not store your login credentials or any other personal information from these services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the Strava Athlete ID you provide to match songs to your activities. If you have enabled the auto-upload feature, MoovIt will automatically upload your song list to your Strava activity description. You can turn off this feature at any time by signing in to MoovIt.

3. Third-Party Services

MoovIt interacts with third-party services such as Spotify and Strava to provide you with our services. Please review their respective privacy policies to understand how they handle your data:

  • Spotify Privacy Policy
  • Strava Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at harbirbajwa[at]hotmail[dot]com.
